# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard Analysis 🛒

## Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and revenue growth to support strategic business decisions.

This project helps business owners, sales managers, and decision-makers understand customer purchasing patterns, optimize product performance, and improve profitability using data-driven insights.

The dashboard provides a centralized analytical view of business performance for better operational planning and revenue optimization.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses often struggle to identify high-performing products, customer buying patterns, and revenue leakage due to scattered sales data.

Manual analysis slows down business decisions and affects growth opportunities.

This dashboard solves that problem by providing a complete business intelligence solution for tracking sales performance and customer behavior.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Monitor total sales performance
* Analyze customer purchase behavior
* Identify top-performing products
* Improve revenue optimization
* Track order and profit trends
* Support strategic business growth
